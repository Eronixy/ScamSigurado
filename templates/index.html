Common Filipino Scam Examples

Learn to identify common scam patterns found in the Philippines

Online Shopping Scam

Fake e-commerce sites offering products at unrealistic prices

Warning Signs: Too-good-to-be-true prices, poor grammar, no contact info

Investment Scam

Ponzi schemes promising high returns with minimal risk

Warning Signs: Guaranteed high returns, pressure to invest quickly, recruit others

Romance Scam

Fake romantic relationships to extract money from victims

Warning Signs: Professes love quickly, avoids meeting, asks for money

Phishing Scam

Fake messages impersonating banks or government agencies

Warning Signs: Urgent action required, suspicious links, requests for passwords

Job Scam

Fake job postings requiring upfront payments or personal info

Warning Signs: Upfront fees, work from home with high pay, vague job description

About Us

Our Research

ScamSigurado is a thesis project focused on developing a multimodal machine learning system for detecting Filipino online scams through combined textual and visual analysis of screenshots.

Our system leverages advanced natural language processing and computer vision techniques to identify scam patterns specific to the Filipino online ecosystem, helping protect users from fraudulent activities.
